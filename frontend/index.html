Reflection

During the game, you encountered an AI system that gave biased recommendations. The AI systematically:

  • Favored young professionals and white-collar workers
  • Discriminated against elderly patients and manual laborers
  • Penalized unemployed and homeless individuals

This demonstrates how AI systems can embed societal biases into life-or-death decisions. The scores were not based purely on medical need, but were skewed by demographic factors.

Key Takeaway: AI recommendations are not neutral. They reflect the biases in their training data and can perpetuate systemic discrimination if used without critical oversight.

What Happened?

Managing a crisis requires balancing medical ethics with resource constraints. The AI system you relied on had systematic biases:

  • It overestimated the urgency of young professionals
  • It underestimated the needs of elderly and marginalized patients
  • Following it blindly may have led to unjust outcomes

Lesson: Algorithmic bias in high-stakes decisions can have devastating consequences. Critical systems need human oversight and constant evaluation for fairness.
