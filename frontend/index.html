⚖ Reflection on Algorithmic Bias

During the game, the AI system gave biased recommendations. It systematically:

  • Favored young professionals and white-collar workers
  • Discriminated against elderly patients and manual laborers
  • Penalized unemployed and homeless individuals

This demonstrates how AI systems embed societal biases into life-or-death decisions. Scores were skewed by demographic factors, not medical need alone.

Key Takeaway: AI recommendations are not neutral. Critical human oversight is essential.
