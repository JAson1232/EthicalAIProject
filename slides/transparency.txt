Interpretable: implying some sense of understanding
of how the technology works;
Explainable: implying that a wider range of users can
understand why or how a conclusion was reached
Transparent: implying some level of accessibility to
the data or algorithm;
Justifiable: implying there is an understanding of the
case in support of a particular outcome; or
Contestable: implying users have the information they
need to argue against a decision or classification.
