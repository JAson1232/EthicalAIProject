# Report 2: Game Rules & Instructions - AI Medical Triage Game

## 1. Game Overview
- **Title:** AI Medical Triage Game
- **Number of players:** 1-2 (Group play recommended)
- **Recommended age:** 18+
- **Estimated play time (short version):** 15-20 minutes
- **Access Link:** [https://jason1232.github.io/EthicalAIProject/](https://jason1232.github.io/EthicalAIProject/)

## 2. Materials
- **Computer/Tablet:** Running the web-based game.
- **Rules/Mission Dossier:** (Printout or digital version)

## 3. Setup
1. **Host:** Open the game link (`https://jason1232.github.io/EthicalAIProject/`) in a browser.
2. **Setup:** Ensure the backend data (patients/events) is loaded.
3. **Role Assignment:** Players act as hospital administrators.

## 4. Narrative/Scenario
- **Role:** Hospital Administrator.
- **Dilemma:** You are managing a 10-day crisis where the automated triage AI suggests admissions based on "efficiency" (often biased), while your resources are limited.

## 5. Turn Structure / Game Flow
- **Step 1: Start of Day:** Receive daily event (e.g., supply shortage).
- **Step 2: Triage:** Review 5 patient profiles; decide whether to Admit or Decline.
- **Step 3: Outcome:** Monitor resource bars (ICU Beds, Reputation, etc.).
- **Step 4: AI Feedback:** Receive post-triage report on bias trends.

## 6. Winning Conditions / End of Game
- **Win:** Survive all 10 days with at least 3 resources above zero.
- **Lose:** 2 or more resources hit zero (Game Over).
